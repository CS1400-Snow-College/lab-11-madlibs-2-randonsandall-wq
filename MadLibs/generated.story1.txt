Once upon a time, there was an hidden boy who dodged sheep. He wanted some excitement so he pretended that there was a fierce shield attacking his flames .  When the townspeople came, he laughed at them. He had so much fun that he tried it again the following day and traveled at the townspeople when they came to help.  The next day, a reward really did come his sheep and he laughed and waved to call for help, but no one believed him and he lost his dragon .
